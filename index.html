About me

Yatra ab tak ki

5 Years ago, Vivek left allopathy to teach Yoga and meditation because prevention is better than cure. Spreading smile and making people healthier and happier are the goals of his life. He has been teaching at Ashoka University since 2017. He has taken workshops during orientation week, bridge program, alumni meet-ups. He conducts regular classes for students and faculty with a focus on mind, sleep, food, and science behind yoga and meditation. He is a certified Yoga Faculty by Quality Council of India (AYUSH Ministry) and The Art of Living Yoga.   In 2017, on International Yoga Day he was sent to Europe as an Ambassador of Yoga by ICCR, Ministry of External Affairs. He was also invited as a speaker at the European House, Zagreb on the Yoga Symposium. He took yoga classes in Drug Deaddiction Centre at Chandigarh and has also facilitated yoga classes for one thousand youths in different cities viz Rijeka, Zagreb, Delhi, Chandigarh, Mumbai, Bhopal, Jabalpur, and Sagar.
